Services / Painting Services

Painting Services

Interior and exterior painting with clean lines and lasting results.

Schedule this service

Overview

Transform any room or the entire exterior of your home with our professional painting services. We prep surfaces meticulously and use premium paints for a durable, beautiful finish.

What's included

  • No drips, streaks, or messy cleanups
  • We protect floors and furniture with drop cloths
  • Color consultation available to help you choose

How it works

1

Preparation

We move furniture, tape edges, patch holes, and sand surfaces for a smooth base.

2

Priming & Painting

We apply primer if needed, then two coats of high-quality paint for even coverage.

3

Cleanup

We remove tape, touch up edges, and leave the room spotless.

Common questions

Do you provide paint or do I need to buy it?

We can provide paint or work with paint you’ve already purchased. We recommend premium brands for best results.

How long does it take to paint a room?

A standard 12x12 room typically takes one to two days, including drying time between coats.
