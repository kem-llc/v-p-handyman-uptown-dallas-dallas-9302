Our story

Your Trusted Neighborhood Handyman

To provide Uptown Dallas homeowners with reliable, high-quality handyman services that make home maintenance easy and stress-free.

V & P Handyman Uptown Dallas was founded with a simple mission: bring professional, honest handyman services to our own neighborhood. We saw too many homeowners struggling with unreliable contractors or overpriced repairs, and we knew we could do better. Starting with a few basic tools and a commitment to quality, we built a reputation for showing up on time, communicating clearly, and doing the job right the first time.

Today, we’re proud to serve hundreds of families across Uptown Dallas, from the State Thomas area to West Village and beyond. Our team has grown, but our values haven’t changed. We still treat every home like it’s our own, and we’re not satisfied until you are.
