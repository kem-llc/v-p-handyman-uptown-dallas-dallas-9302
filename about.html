Our story

Your Trusted Handyman in Uptown Dallas

To provide Uptown Dallas homeowners with reliable, high-quality handyman services that make home maintenance easy and stress-free.

V & P Handyman Uptown Dallas was founded with a simple mission: deliver honest, skilled craftsmanship to our neighbors. With over 15 years of combined experience, our team has grown from fixing small repairs to handling full-scale renovations. We take pride in being the go-to handyman for Uptown residents who value quality and reliability.

Every project, big or small, receives the same level of care and attention. We believe in building lasting relationships through transparency, fair pricing, and exceptional work. When you hire V&P, you're not just getting a handyman — you're getting a partner who cares about your home as much as you do.
